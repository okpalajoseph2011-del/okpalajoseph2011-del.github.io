The silver clock hummed quietly in the corner of the room as shadows danced across the walls like whispers of forgotten dreams. A small breeze carried the scent of rain and old paper through the cracked window, stirring the pages of a journal left open on the desk. Beneath a flickering lamp, a curious note was scrawled in the margin: “Follow the sound of the bell—not the echo.” No one knew who wrote it, or why the ink shimmered faintly under moonlight.
