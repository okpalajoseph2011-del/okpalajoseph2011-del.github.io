About the Show

Rick and Morty is an animated sci-fi comedy about the adventures of a mad scientist Rick and his grandson Morty as they travel across dimensions and timelines. It's packed with humor, satire, and bizarre scenarios.

Main Characters

  • Rick Sanchez – Genius scientist, alcoholic, chaotic.
  • Morty Smith – Rick's anxious but good-hearted grandson.
  • Summer Smith – Morty's older sister, smart and ambitious.
  • Beth Smith – Rick's daughter, a horse surgeon.
  • Jerry Smith – Morty's insecure dad.
